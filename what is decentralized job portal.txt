A true decentralized job portal is a platform that utilizes blockchain technology to connect job seekers with job opportunities without the need for a central authority or intermediary. It operates on a decentralized network, where all transactions are recorded on a public ledger, creating transparency and security. The platform utilizes smart contracts to automate the process of matching job seekers with job opportunities, eliminating the need for intermediaries and reducing costs. Users have full control over their personal data, and the platform operates on a peer-to-peer basis, providing a more direct and efficient way for job seekers and employers to connect. A true decentralized job portal is not controlled by any single entity, it is self-governed and operates on a consensus mechanism, where all the users have a say in the rules and regulations of the platform.
